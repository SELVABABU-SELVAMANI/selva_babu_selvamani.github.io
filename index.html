About Me

My academic journey and research interests in bioinformatics.

I am a dedicated bioinformatics researcher with a Master of Science in Agricultural Bioinformatics (CGPA: 8.69) and a Bachelor of Technology in Bioinformatics (CGPA: 8.15) from Tamil Nadu Agricultural University, Coimbatore.

Currently serving as a Senior Research Fellow at the Division of Genomic Resources, ICAR-National Bureau of Agricultural Insect Resources, Bangalore, I focus on NGS data analysis for exploring rapid speciation and adaptation in agricultural pests. My research encompasses genomics, transcriptomics, and metagenomics analysis.

With over 22 published research papers in reputed international journals (several with high impact factors exceeding 8.5) and extensive experience in both computational and wet-lab techniques, I am passionate about bridging the gap between biological questions and computational solutions.

Research Projects

Academic and professional research contributions in bioinformatics and genomics.

Genomics

Genome Assembly & Annotation

Assembled genomes of insects and microbes using advanced tools. Performed comprehensive annotation and identified genes of interest for wet-lab validation.

Transcriptomics

Drought-Responsive miRNAs in Rice

Identified miRNAs playing crucial roles in abiotic stress mechanisms through bioinformatics analysis of small RNA sequencing data at ICAR-NIPB.

Metabolomics

Colchicine Biosynthesis

Performed colchicine profiling using HPLC/HPTLC. Conducted transcriptome analysis to identify key genes and transcription factors in the biosynthesis pathway.

Metagenomics

Microbiome Analysis

Conducted comprehensive metagenomics analysis from quality trimming to microbial taxonomy identification using advanced bioinformatics pipelines.

QTL Analysis

Drought Tolerance QTLs

Elucidated genes in QTL regions (qDTY1.1, qEMF3, qDTY2.1, qDTY3.1) through RNA-Seq analysis and pathway ontology studies.

Molecular Genetics

SSR-based DNA Fingerprinting

Developed and validated SSR markers to differentiate little millet varieties for seed bank registration.
