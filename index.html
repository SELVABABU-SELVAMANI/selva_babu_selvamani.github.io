Academic Background

I am a dedicated bioinformatics researcher with specialized expertise in genomics, transcriptomics, and computational biology. My academic journey has been marked by excellence and a deep commitment to advancing our understanding of biological systems through computational approaches.

Previously served as a Senior Research Fellow at the Division of Genomic Resources, ICAR-National Bureau of Agricultural Insect Resources in Bangalore (completed January 2026), where I focused on NGS data analysis for exploring rapid speciation and adaptation in agricultural pests. My research encompassed comprehensive omics data analysis, with particular emphasis on identifying genes for dsRNA design in RNAi applications and insecticidal resistance studies.
