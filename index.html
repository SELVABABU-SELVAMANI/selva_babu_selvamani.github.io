Academic Background

I am a dedicated bioinformatics researcher with specialized expertise in genomics, transcriptomics, and computational biology. My academic journey has been marked by excellence and a deep commitment to advancing our understanding of biological systems through computational approaches.

Currently serving as a Senior Research Fellow at the Division of Genomic Resources, ICAR-National Bureau of Agricultural Insect Resources in Bangalore, I focus on NGS data analysis for exploring rapid speciation and adaptation in agricultural pests. My research encompasses comprehensive omics data analysis, with particular emphasis on identifying genes for dsRNA design in RNAi applications and insecticidal resistance studies.

Research

Academic Projects

Computational and experimental research in genomics, transcriptomics, and systems biology

01
Genomics

Genome Assembly & Annotation

Comprehensive genome assembly of insects and microbes using state-of-the-art tools including MaSurca, CANU, and HiFiasm. Performed functional annotation and identified genes of interest for downstream wet-lab validation studies.

02
Transcriptomics

Drought-Responsive miRNAs

Identified microRNAs playing crucial roles in abiotic stress mechanisms in drought-tolerant rice varieties through comprehensive small RNA sequencing analysis at ICAR-National Institute for Plant Biotechnology, New Delhi.

03
Metabolomics

Colchicine Biosynthesis

Elucidated colchicine biosynthesis pathway in Gloriosa superba using HPLC/HPTLC profiling combined with transcriptome analysis. Identified key genes, miRNAs, and transcription factors regulating secondary metabolite production.

04
Metagenomics

Microbiome Analysis

Conducted comprehensive metagenomic analysis of soil, agricultural formulations, and insect gut microbiomes. Implemented quality control, taxonomic classification, and abundance estimation using advanced bioinformatics pipelines.

05
QTL Mapping

Drought Tolerance QTLs

Elucidated putative candidate genes underlying drought tolerance QTLs (qDTY1.1, qEMF3, qDTY2.1, qDTY3.1) in rice through pathway analysis and trait ontology. Validated gene expression patterns via RNA-Seq analysis.

06
Molecular Genetics

SSR-based Fingerprinting

Developed and validated novel SSR molecular markers for characterizing little millet germplasm. Successfully differentiated newly released varieties from existing accessions for seed bank registration.
